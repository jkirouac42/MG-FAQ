Frequently Asked Questions



  • S3D Print Quality troubleshooting guide?

    Simplify3D has come up with a great print troubleshooting guide. S3D Print Troubleshooting Guide.

  • How do I clear or prevent a clog?

    For clog prevention and clearing we suggest trying a Cold Pull. The idea is to get old filament buildup order debris to stick to the
    filament so it can be extracted out of the hotend assembly. Check our our
    article on how to perform a Cold Pull.

  • How should I take care of my build surface?

    We suggest cleaning your bed, in between prints, with 99% isopropyl alcohol. If you need to replace you polyimide file check out our article on Bed Prep and Maintenance.

  • What is Simplify3D?

    Simplify3D is a software program used in combination with the MakerGear 3D printers for preparing dual-extrusion print jobs. What is Simplify3D?

  • Where do I get the S3D profiles for the M3?

    Simplify3D profiles for your MakerGear M3 printer are available on our MakerGear Knowledge Base.
    Simplify3D profiles for your MakerGear M3.

  • How do I setup a single extruder print with a M3ID?

    You can set of a single extruder print in Simplify3d by selecting Single Mode
    in the Auto-Configure Extruders section of your FFF settings. Check out the article
    here. Single Extrusion prints with the M3ID.

  • How do I setup a Dual color print with my M3ID?

    Dual color prints can be set up by setting up separate processess for your extruders. Check out our article and video on how to set it up.
    Dual color printing with the M3ID.

  • How do I setup a Duplication Mode print?

    Duplication mode requires two steps. First, you physically need to calibrate the hotends for duplication mode prints. Second you need to slice your project for Dual extruders with Simplify3D
